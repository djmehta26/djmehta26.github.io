WHY TRAINSPOTTING IS THE GREATEST MOVIE OF THE 90's

Trainspotting is a dark comedy made in 1996 directed by Danny Boyle who is a scottish director known for directing other movies such as Slumdog Millionaire, 28 days later, 127 hours.

This movie addresses urban proverty and drug-addiction specifically heroin in Edinburg Scotland.
